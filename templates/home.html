Annual loss

Globally, due to soil erosion, the annual amount of fertilizers mobilized is equivalent to 34 US$ billion for N and 80 US$ billion for P which is an important financial loss

Human Health

Plant diseases are well known to reduce the food available to humans by ultimately interfering with crop yields. This can result in inadequate food to humans or lead to starvation and death in the worst cases

Threat to Aquatic life

As excess fertilizers enter aquatic ecosystems and cause algal blooms, the algae die off more rapidly. As algae decompose, they remove oxygen from the water, according to the Natural Resources Defense Council and the EPA. A lack of oxygen in the water then kills off fish and other aquatic life
